About AFKmate

Your AI coding companion that works while you're away.

Our Mission

We believe that every developer deserves an AI assistant that works as hard as they do - even when they step away from the keyboard.

AFKmate was born from a simple observation: developers spend significant time away from their code - grabbing coffee, attending meetings, or just taking a well-deserved break. What if that time could be productive?

What We Do

AFKmate automatically analyzes your code when you're idle, finding bugs, security vulnerabilities, and improvement opportunities. When you return, actionable insights are waiting for you.

Our AI-powered analysis understands context, recognizes patterns, and provides suggestions that actually make sense for your codebase.
