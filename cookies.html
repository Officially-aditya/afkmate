Cookie Policy

How we use cookies on the AFKmate website.

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you interact with the site.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session cookies: Remember your login state
  • Security cookies: Help protect against unauthorized access

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics to understand how visitors use our website:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Traffic sources
  • Device and browser information

This data is anonymized and helps us improve the website.

Preference Cookies

These remember your choices:

  • Theme preferences
  • Cookie consent choice

Third-Party Cookies

We may use services that set their own cookies:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Analytics providers: For website statistics

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Allow cookies from specific sites only

Note that blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.

The VS Code Extension

The AFKmate VS Code extension does not use cookies. It stores preferences locally in VS Code's settings storage.
